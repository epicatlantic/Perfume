Batch Subscription Deliveries

Choose one fixed date — the 1st or the 15th — as your Subscription Delivery Day. Every subscriber's package ships at exactly the same time, reducing logistics overhead.

Direct Sale Window

Meet the Founder

The person behind the scent

Founder & Chief Executive Officer
"Fragrance is the one luxury that doesn't ask you to look a certain way. It asks you to feel a certain way — and that feeling should be available to everyone."

ScentFlow was born from a simple frustration: premium fragrances were either locked behind steep one-time prices or buried in confusing subscription models. We built a dual-track system that gives customers the freedom to choose how they want to own their scent journey — and rewards them for every step of it.

Based in Lagos, we source the finest fragrance oils, hand-fill every mist in small batches, and ship across Nigeria with the care of a boutique — at a price that makes sense.

— LADE
